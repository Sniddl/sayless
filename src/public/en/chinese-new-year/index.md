---
title: Chinese New Year
---

Chinese New Year marks the start of the lunar new year, usually between January 21 and February 20. Each year is associated with one of the 12 zodiac animals in a repeating cycle:

* Rat
* Ox
* Tiger
* Rabbit
* Dragon (2024)
* Snake
* Horse
* Goat
* Monkey
* Rooster
* Dog
* Pig
